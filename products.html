PMHNP-BC Samantha Heim

Mental Healthcare has been my passion since I was young. It is my aim to provide understanding and validation of suffering, to dispense hope and warmth in abundance, and to support those who feel most misunderstood and underrepresented in our society. During my years working as a psychiatric inpatient nurse, I was sad that there were hardly any providers in general and even fewer who see children. The providers that do see patients give cold and fast service, leaving many symptoms unattended and many questions unasked. I am happy to start this private practice to serve our most vulnerable population because everyone deserves a second chance to start life on the right foot. I look forward to hearing your story.

PMHNP-BC Tabitha Imondi

I am a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner who uses a holistic approach to care. While I believe psychotropic medication can be both curative and beneficial for symptom management, I encourage my patients to engage meaningfully in their own treatment through self-care and personal wellness activities. Our time together will include development of plans to incorporate such wellness activities, that will foster independence and a sense of empowerment, as physical health, nutrition, sleep hygiene, and social supports are all essential to mental wellness. My focus of treatment is to enhance the adaptive, healing capability of each individual, and symptoms will be assessed using a biopsychosocial framework.
